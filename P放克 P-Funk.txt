P放克（P-Funk，P Funk 或 P. Funk）为一种由乔治．克林顿（George Clinton）以及其率领的Parliament-Funkadelic乐团所主导的音乐类型，全盛期约于1970年代中期，后来并广为许多的hip hop音乐家采样，许多的现场乐队亦继续表演此类型之音乐，使得P-Funk得以继续于视听大众间继续流传。P-Funk的词源得由许多不同的方面解释。有数种不同的说法，分别指为“Parliament-Funkadelic”、“纯放克”（pure funk）、或是“Plainfield Funk”，指美国新泽西州之 Plainfield 市，也就是该乐团的发迹地。 Motor Booty Affair专辑之CD版本内页说明指出"P"这个字母解释做“纯粹”（pure）。由于P-Funk极为广受欢迎，因此现在成为了单独的音乐形式。此类音乐的乐迷通常称作"The P"。P-Funk 不宜与Rick James以及 Stone City 乐团擅长的punk funk混淆，尽管这是由Rick James受P-Funk启发而来的音乐形式。基本特征P-Funk的鲜明特点包括单一肥厚的鼓声、迷幻恍惚的合成器声、深沉的贝斯声、常常偏重的吉他。流行时段1970年代中期英文简介P-Funk is a distinctive sub-style of Funk created in the mid-1970s by George Clinton and his fellow musicians of Parliament and Funkadelic fame, among others. Characteristics are a "fat", monotonic drum sound; spaced-out synthesizers; thumping, "squelchy" basslines; tight, syncopated horn sections; and sometimes heavy guitars. This sound has been brought by former Clinton collaborators to many spin-off bands, for example Bootsy's Rubber Band and Jerome Brailey's Mutiny. P-Funk has been a fruitful source of sampling for Hip Hop acts since the late 1980s.
