法国流行是以法语演绎的一种流行音乐，常见于法国、比利时、魁北克等法语地区的歌手。French pop music is the pop music sung in the French language. It is usually performed by singers from France, Belgium, Quebec, or any of the other francophone areas of the world. The target audience is the francophone market (primarily France), which is considerably smaller and largely independent from the mainstream anglophone market.
